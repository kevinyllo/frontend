Hong Kong people are paying more and more attention to health, and running is a sport that many people love. It is simple and convenient, and does not require any equipment or equipment. In addition, there are many running paths and seasides in Hong Kong for runners to run. However, just because running is not difficult, many people ignore the correct posture and precautions before and after running, which increases the chance of injury during running. Here is an article to let you know some running precautions to avoid accidental injuries.

Be careful when preparing to run

1. Keep an eye on running times and routes
Everyone's physique and physical strength are different, so you need to adjust the length of running time according to your own ability and state, and proceed step by step. It is also recommended to choose a familiar route or venue for safer running.

2. Need to warm up before running
Don't underestimate the importance of warming up. Sufficient warm-up before running can help warm up the body, promote blood circulation, improve muscle endurance, increase flexibility, and reduce the chance of running injuries.

3. Wear proper running shoes
Not all sports shoes are suitable for running. A good pair of sports shoes needs to match the runner's foot shape, weight and running experience to avoid injuries during running.


Precautions during running

1. Pay attention to running posture
Running posture is very important. Once the posture is wrong, incorrect running posture for a long time will cause damage to joints and ligaments, and will increase the chance of sprains.
*For details, please refer to the Leisure and Cultural Services Department’s recommendation.

2. Pay attention to your physical condition
When running, you need to pay attention to your physical condition, including heartbeat and breathing, especially those with health problems. You should seek advice from a doctor or other professionals before running.

3. Hydration
During running, you need to replenish water regularly to avoid dehydration during exercise, and you can also replenish glucose and electrolytes according to individual needs.


Post-Run Advice

1. Do not sit or lie down immediately
During running, blood is mainly sent to the limbs, and the muscles of the limbs are in a state of contraction, sending blood back to the heart, and the heart contracts and sends it back to the limbs. If you sit or lie down immediately after running, the blood vessels in the extremities suddenly relax, and there is not enough blood to flow back to the heart, and shock may occur.

2. Remember to stretch
Don’t overlook the importance of stretching after running. Stretching can help relieve tense muscles during running. It can also enhance the flexibility of muscles and joints, and reduce the chance of muscle soreness and inflammation.

3. Do not eat immediately
When running, the function of our digestive system will be weakened, and it will take some time to recover after running. Therefore, it is not suitable to eat immediately, which may easily cause indigestion or stomach pain.
